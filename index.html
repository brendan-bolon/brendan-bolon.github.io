Blog Posts

At the start of the semester I created three blog posts. The post's examine different subjects in the world of technology.

My first post examines the possible integration of blockchain into the stock market.
My second post looks at the city of Austin Texas as possibly the next hub for technical innovation or "Silicon Valley".
My third post examines the negative effects of the app Tapingo on Virginia Tech and it's students.

Podcast

I also worked with four other students to create a Podcast. Since we are all Computer Science students that had always heard about why Computer Science was such a good major, we decieded to argue the contrary.

The audio of the Podcast can be found here.

Video

I also worked in a group to make a video about the unkown and possibly dangerous future of Artifical Intelligence (or A.I.). I had a lot of fun making this video and am very happy with the result. Making a video was something very new to me and I hope you enjoy it!
